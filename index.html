SALARPURIA RHYTHM:

Salarpuria Sattva Group welcomes you to its upcoming residential prelaunch of Salarpuria Rhythm near Gopalan Arcade Mall, Mysore Road, Bangalore. This property in Mysore road is coming up on an 11 acre of land with its expected possession to feature soon in the coming years. This is a worthy option for great investment returns in forthcoming future. Spreading about 8.5 acre of residential and 2.5 acre of commercial land with 8 multiplex, Salarpuria Rhythm offers 2, 3 and 4 BHK apartments of sizes 1100 SQFT onwards. These apartments are vastu compatible and produce appropriate ventilation inside each& every proposed unit. Meeting global standards and adorned with a host of amenities, this project provides you easy commute to key regions of Bangalore. Here your home is surrounded by lush greenery, open spaces and gives you an over whelming feeling with enough space to sprawl out.

Salarpuria Rhythm has broad prominent pathways, a large swimming pool, club houses, gymnasium, and an indoor game area. Located on Pantharapalya, Raja Rajeshwari Nagar, Mysore Road, Nayandahalli, Bangalore, Salarpuria Rythm is in close proximity to proposed IT corridor, NICE corridor, ring road, specialty hospital, shopping complexes and reputed schools. Here specifications are lavishly designed, elegantly furnished and laced with a space to million dollar outstanding views and comforts. This prelaunch on Mysore Road also conforms to all safety standards and the building structure is totally earthquake-resistant. By way of luxurious adoptions with calm, comfort and coziness, Salarpuria Rhythm is a warm and welcoming prelaunch for Bangalore city lovers.
